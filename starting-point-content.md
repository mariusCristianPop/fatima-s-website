# BUSINESS ON THE HEELS


Founded by Fatima, the club was established with a clear mission: to create a
strong, supportive, and strategic community where ambitious women can grow
their businesses with confidence and structure.


This is more than networking.

This is leadership development, collaboration, and economic empowerment.


Business on the Heels provides:

• Strategic business guidance

• Educational workshops and masterclasses

• Networking opportunities

• Mentorship and accountability

• A powerful community of like-minded women


The club was built on the belief that women can lead with strength, elegance, and
intelligence — while building profitable and sustainable businesses.


Through connection, knowledge, and action, Business on the Heels helps women
move from ambition to achievement.


This is not just about business.

It is about legacy, independence, and impact.
